League Rules & Conduct

📋 Official Tournament Guidelines • Fair Play • Good Sportsmanship

⚖️ Tournament Governance

These rules ensure fair play, maintain pace, and guarantee everyone has an awesome time on the course!

🎯 Remember: When in doubt, play it fair and keep the game moving. Tournament officials have final say on all rulings.

⏱️ Pace of Play

Keep the Game Moving:

  • 🏃‍♂️ Stay with the Group Ahead: Keep up with the group in front of you, not just ahead of the group behind
  • ⏰ 9-Hole Time Limit: Complete each 9-hole segment in under 2 hours
  • 🔍 Lost Ball Search: Maximum search time is 3 minutes - then take relief
  • ⚡ Ready Golf: If you're safe and ready, hit! Don't wait for honor system

💡 Pace of Play Tips:

On the Tee: Have your club ready, be thinking about your shot
Approaching: Take multiple clubs, read your lie while walking
On the Green: Line up putts while others are putting

🏌️ Lie & Relief Rules

📍 Lie Improvement:

  • 📏 Placement Rule: Within 1 club length of selected shot position
  • 🌱 Same Cut: Fairway-to-fairway, rough-to-rough, sand-to-sand
  • 🚫 No Cross-Conditions: No placing on fringe/green if original was rough, and vice versa

🚧 Unplayable Lies:

🆓 Free Relief Situations:

  • 🌳 Base of a tree
  • 🪵 Exposed root
  • 💧 Standing water (not casual water)
  • 🌿 Embedded in thick vegetation

→ Free relief within 1 club length, same condition

⚠️ Does NOT Qualify: Light rough or tree lines do not qualify for free relief

🚩 Out of Bounds / Lost Ball:

  • ⚡ Lateral OB: 1-stroke penalty, drop within 2 club lengths of boundary last crossed, no closer to hole
  • 🏊‍♂️ Hole 7 Exception: If both players are in the pond, re-tee with 1-stroke penalty (stroke-and-distance)

🚧 Ground Under Repair

Free Relief Guidelines:

  • 🆓 Free Drop: Within 1 club length from marked areas
  • 🏷️ Identification: Use course markings or group consensus

🔍 What Qualifies as Ground Under Repair:

Marked Areas
White-lined zones
Construction
Active work zones
Damage
Obvious course damage

⛳ Putting Surface

Green Rules & Etiquette:

  • 🧽 Mark and Clean: Always mark your ball position before cleaning
  • 🎯 Team Putting: Both players may putt; if either makes it, you're in!
  • ✅ Hole Out: All putts must be holed unless within putter grip length and conceded

🏌️ Putting Etiquette Tips:

  • Don't step on putting lines
  • Repair ball marks and divots
  • Replace flagstick when everyone is done
  • Be ready when it's your turn

🤝 Conduct & Sportsmanship

🏆 Remember the Golden Rule

Play the game with integrity, treat others with respect, and have a blast doing it!

📋 Quick Rules Reference

⏱️ Pace: 2 hours per 9 holes
📏 Placement: 1 club length, same cut
🔍 Lost Ball: 3 minutes max search
⚡ Ready Golf: Hit when safe and ready
🏊‍♂️ Hole 7: Both in pond = re-tee +1
⛳ Putting: Mark, clean, hole out
